üéØ How to Add Products

  1. Select Product Type: Choose from Clothing, Household Items, Electronics, etc.
  2. Choose Category: Pick a relevant subcategory (automatically populated based on type)
  3. Enter Product Details:
    • Name (e.g., "Polo T-Shirt" or "Dinner Plate Set")
    • Brand, SKU, Barcode
    • HSN/SAC code and GST %
    • MRP and Sale Price
  4. Add Variants (Optional):
    • For Clothing: Sizes (S, M, L) and Colors
    • For Household: Capacity (500ml, 1L) and Material
    • For Electronics: Storage (64GB, 128GB) and Colors
    • Leave empty for single-variant items
  5. Generate Variants: Click to preview all combinations
  6. Save Product: Click "Save Product" button
